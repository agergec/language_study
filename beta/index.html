🚀 Getting Started

Load a word list by dragging one or more .txt files onto the page, or by clicking the welcome screen to browse your files.

🎮 Main Controls

  • Next (Space): Proceeds to the next word.
  • Repeat (R): Repeats the audio for the current word.
  • Show (S): Instantly reveals the text of the current word.

🧩 Privative Prefix Quiz

When your list has words that begin with un or une, enable the Privative Prefix mode in Settings. You will see the base word like “… ami”. Choose the correct prefix with the buttons. If you skip, the answer is recorded as “… ami” in Answers.

⚙️ Settings

Click the gear icon to manage settings. You can choose from different study modes, select voices, and enable/disable your loaded word lists for the current session. Use “Test Voice” to preview the current voice.

↔️ Panels

On desktop, you can click the ‹ icon on the "Answers" panel to collapse and expand it.
